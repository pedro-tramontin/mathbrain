A simple, distraction-free tool to practice mental math.

## Description

Keep your brain sharp with MathBrain.

Sometimes, you don't need fancy graphics or complicated game modes. You just need to practice. MathBrain is the simplest way to improve your mental math skills, one equation at a time.

How it works: The app is designed for pure focus. It works in a simple loop:

1. The Challenge: The app generates a random math expression.
2. Your Input: You calculate the answer and type it in.
3. The Result: Click the button to see if you are right or wrong.
4. Repeat: Move instantly to the next challenge.

Why use MathBrain?

- Endless Practice: The challenges are generated randomly, so you never run out of problems to solve.
- Instant Feedback: Know immediately if you got the answer right so you can learn and improve.
- Lightweight: The app takes up very little space on your phone and loads instantly.

Perfect for:
- Students wanting to practice arithmetic speed.
- Adults looking to keep their mind active.
- Anyone who wants to kill time productively without a complex game.

Download MathBrain today and start exercising your mind!
